About

    SB design is a website to show of my photography, Illustration and art project that I made. SB is came from "Sara Burke", my name.

    I learned Illustration and arts from my college, I love everything about arts. I started an art work since I notice that coloring book and color pencils are exist. I was doing it as a hobby until I got in college. I learned more about media art. I got to try tools that make my art projects easier. I had learned drawing, painting, exhibition, film, animation, photography, and graphic arts.

    My art work, much like my life, it came out of my feeling, everystrokes has feeling itself, made the painting look alive.

    I love taking photos. I have camera with me everywhere, because you never find the special moment all the time. Everytime when I take photos, I learned from every photos that I took. I felt free while I am taking photos.

    For my last years of college I picked to learn interactive project by myself, and made a project to telling some information of "Sgt. Maj. Thawee Folk museum" by using tools called Adobe Flash and Adobe Illustrator programs. I realized at that time I really love to do interactive design.

    I made this website to learn programmimg by using tools called HTML&CSS, Javascript and Git. I have learned by myself from internet site to answer all of my questions.
